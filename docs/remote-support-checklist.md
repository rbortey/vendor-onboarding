# Remote Support Checklist

Use this checklist before scheduling a vendor remote support session.

## Before the Window

- Confirm the support contact and company name.
- Confirm the business owner for the system.
- Confirm whether the vendor needs portal access, remote support access, or file exchange only.
- Record the ticket or change reference.
- Confirm whether MFA is enabled for any portal account involved.

## During the Window

- Keep support activity inside the approved scope.
- Avoid reusing shared credentials across systems.
- Watch for repeated MFA prompts and confirm with the named user before approval.
- Ask the vendor to state when work is complete.

## After the Window

- Disable or expire temporary access.
- Close the service desk ticket only after the business owner confirms completion.
- Preserve relevant logs for the support window.
